Paradise

Paradise Biryani is an iconic restaurant in Hyderabad known for serving authentic Hyderabadi biryani since 1953. It has gained a legendary reputation for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and perfectly cooked meat, making it a must-visit for locals and tourists alike.

Veg Items 

- Paneer Tikka
- Veg Biryani
- Gobi Manchurian
- Veg Kebab Platter
- Dal Tadka

Non-Veg Items:

- Chicken Biryani
- Mutton Biryani
- Chicken Tikka Masala
- Fish Fry
- Kebab Platter

Special:

Paradise's signature Hyderabadi Mutton Biryani stands out for its perfectly marinated meat cooked with fragrant rice, offering a rich and satisfying taste.

Feedback:

Customers appreciate Paradise for its consistent taste, generous portions, and quick service. However, during peak hours, there may be long waiting times, so early visits are recommended.
